Lasagna Recipe

You Ain't Neva Had Lasagna Like Dis Befo'

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna

Steps To Making This Tasty Lasagna

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Perpare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer and bake
